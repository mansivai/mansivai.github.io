ABOUT-US

This Book Cafe is based coffee shop that aims to bring out the culture of networking and productive connectivity of human spirits over coffee They also aim to build cafe culture and develop community-building cafes in tier three and two cities and then move to metro cities This cafe, opened in 2016, selects the vendors for coffee beans who promote Indian coffee farmers, create events to promote budding entrepreneurs and local artists and help women to take a stand and develop themselves.

FEATURES

As a customer you will also be able to browse through any of the several hundred local as well as foreign books, magazines and newspapers which will be available there. It is in a book café where you will be able to refuel yourself while getting lost in the City of Books. You can also enjoy an exotic array of food options made fresh daily. Those of you wish to do their reading online will be able to plug in and reap the benefits of the café’s free WI-fi.
